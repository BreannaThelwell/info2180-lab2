Young Women in Tech: Challenging One Pixel At A Time

Women Breaking Barriers in Tech

October 2, 2024 by Breanna Thelwell

Women have been integral to the development of technology. For starters, Ada Lovelace. This is name of the creator of what is considered to be the first computer program. In the mid-1800s, she envisioned the potential of machines beyond mere calculations. In the 1970s and 1980s, more women started entering tech fields. As the industry grew, so did the gender disparities. Despite this, pioneers such as Grace Hopper continued to break barriers by championing computer programming languages.

The topic of diversity has picked up momentum in recent years. Many organisations and initiatives are encouraging women to enter STEM related fields. Although many challenges remain, the impact of women in tech continue to shape the industry, and inspire future generations.

Essential Skills for Women Entering Tech

October 4, 2024 by Breanna Thelwell

There are several essential skills that can help you thrive in this dynamic field. To begin, proficiency in programming languages such as Python or Javascript is crucial for any software development role, and knowledge of user experince (UX) design is invaluable for creating intuitive applications. Additionally, strong problem-solving skills and a solid understanding of data analysis can prop you up in various tech positions. In the tech environment, collaboration drives innovation. Therefore, having soft skills such as effective communication and teamwork are very important.

Finally, cultivating a growing mindset and willigness to learn will continuously empower you to navigate challenges and seize opportunities in your respective tech career.
